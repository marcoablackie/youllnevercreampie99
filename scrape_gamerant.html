Try something different:

NBA 2K26 gives you a ton of freedom when it comes to customizing your player's animations for MyCareer, and you can steal animations from some of the NBA's greatest if you have the right stats and height. There are very specific cutoffs for shooting animations in NBA 2K26, so you'll want to make sure that you give your player the right amount of points before you start MyCareer.

Most of the shooting animations in NBA 2K26 have been ported over from last year's game, but there are a few new ones as well. You can also mix and match shooting animations from different players depending on the selected shot type, so you don't need to create an exact copy of an NBA all-star.
